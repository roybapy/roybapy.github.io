Weatherkast

A beautiful, fast and simple weather app for your Android device.

  • See stunning animation that match time of day and current condition.
  • Import your friends location directly from Facebook.
  • Check the current, hourly and daily forecast with a swipe.
  • Get all the details: wind, pressure, humidity, precipitation, sunrise, sunset, local time.
  • Add and watch all your favorite cities and destinations with no restriction.
